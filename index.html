Welcome

I am a Lecturer in the School of Political Science, Public Affairs, Legal and Security Studies at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley.

My research focuses on public opinion on environmental issue and human-rights based approach to climate change. I am currently research on how rights based approach can change public opinion toward climate change using survey experiment focusing on the political predisposition and gender gap.

I am also a Lead Fellow at the Center of Survey Research and Policy Analysis (CSRPA) at the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley. As a Lead Fellow, I am conducting a project regarding the water issues in Rio Grande Valley, South Texas. Also, participating in diverse survey projects.
